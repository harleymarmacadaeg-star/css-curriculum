What is Applying Quality Standards?

It means performing your work while:

  • Following correct procedures.
  • Using proper materials.
  • Ensuring safety and accuracy.

Standard Operating Procedures (SOP)

What are SOPs?

SOPs are step-by-step instructions that guide technicians on how to perform tasks correctly, safely, and consistently.

Following Work Instructions

When given a work order, a technician must:

1

Read and understand the task instructions.

2

Follow SOPs step-by-step.

3

Use the correct materials and tools.

4

Observe safety procedures.

5

Report issues or defects properly.
