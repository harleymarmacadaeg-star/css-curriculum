Lesson Objectives

At the end of this lesson, learners should be able to:

  • 1 Identify different materials used in computer systems servicing.
  • 2 Describe the basic specifications and uses of wires, cables, and electrical tape.
  • 3 Check received materials against workplace standards and specifications.

A. Wires

Single conductors for internal power and signal connections.

  • • Material: Copper / Aluminum
  • • Insulation: Rubber / Plastic
  • • Rating: AWG (Gauge)

D. Electrical Tape

  • • Quality: High adhesive, no cracks

E. Electronic Components (1/2)

“Found on the Motherboard and Power Supply Unit.”

Resistor

Limits or regulates the flow of electrical current in a circuit.

Capacitor

Stores and releases electrical energy; filters voltage spikes.

IC Chip

A complex set of microscopic circuits on a small silicon chip.

E. Electronic Components (2/2)

“Found on the Motherboard and Power Supply Unit.”

Diode

Allows current to flow in only one direction (one-way valve).

Transistor

Acts as an amplifier or switch. The building block of CPUs.

LO 1.2: Quality Checklist

1. VISUAL

Search for bent pins, burnt marks, or insulation nicks.

2. SPEC

Verify labels: Cat6, AWG, or UL Rating must match J.O.

3. TEST

Perform continuity tests or use a LAN Tester before install.
